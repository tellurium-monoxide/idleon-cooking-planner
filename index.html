IdleOn Cooking Planner

How to use?

This tool will give you the best order to upgrade meals, and give you an estimated time to complete cooking (all meals level max).

Start by pasting your Idleon Efficiency save data below and click the button. This will fill the input fields, do the calculations and display the meal upgrade order.

Then you can manually alter the content of the "Inputs" section, to simulate different stats. To estimate the time, the tool uses your ladles / day production, which is set by default to 10 000 (around what endgamers get). Change this to have a result closer to your real situation. This option is located in Inputs > World 4 > Cooking > Ladles.

After modifying the inputs, you can redo the calculations wiht the button "Compute from inputs" below.
