IdleOn tools

This is a collection of tools to plan and optimize your progression in IdleOn. There is only a single tool for now, but there will be more.

IdleOn Cooking Planner

This tool will give you the best order to upgrade meals (similar to what IdleonToolbox does), and calculates an estimated time to complete cooking (all meals level max), taking into account No Meal Left Behind, equinox cost reduction and ladle production.
